# credit-risk-eda

This case study focuses on evaluating, categorizing, and identifying which borrowers are most likely to default on their loans using a variety of consumer data. The goal is to reduce loan defaults, improve credit risk assessment, and improve customer service by making better educated decisions.

## executive summary 

## business problem 
The core goal is to support lenders in reducing default rates. This involves asking questions such as the following: 

What demographic or borrower profiles are most likely to default?
What is the relationship between dti and default?
What is the correlation between annual income and default?
How do credit grade and subgrade affect default likelihood?
Do loan amount, purpose, interest rate that strongly predict risk?
